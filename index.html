WHAT IS A
GENERATIVE
LOGO?
Generative logos are a dynamic evolution of traditional static logos. Unlike static logos, which are fixed in design and appearance, generative logos are flexible, adaptive and can change form, color, animation based on certain parameters or input.
They are created using algorithms or design systems, allowing them to "move" or transform in response to factors like user interaction, data, or context. This makes them more interactive, modern, and capable of creating a unique experience each time they're seen, while still maintaining the core identity of the brand.
It's important to note that generative logos are not inherently connected to artificial intelligence (AI).
